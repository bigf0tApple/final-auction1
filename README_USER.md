### Bidding Rules

| Rule | Explanation |
|------|-------------|
| **Minimum Bid** | Must be at least **1% higher** than current bid |
| **Maximum Bid** | In final 10 seconds, max is **10% higher** |

## â° Anti-Sniping Protection

**What is sniping?** Placing a bid in the final second so no one can respond.

**Our solution:** If someone bids in the **last 10 seconds**, the auction extends by **10 more seconds**.

```
Example:
- Auction ends at 3:00:00 PM
- Bid placed at 2:59:55 PM (5 seconds left)
- Auction NOW ends at 3:00:05 PM
- Another bid at 3:00:03 PM â†’ extends to 3:00:13 PM
- ...continues until 10 seconds pass with no bids
```

This gives everyone a fair chance to respond!

## â“ FAQ

### Is this real money?

**Right now, NO.** We're on testnet using free test ETH. When we launch on mainnet, real ETH will be used.

### What if I win but can't pay?

You already paid! Bids are on-chain. If you bid 1 ETH, that 1 ETH was sent when you clicked bid.

### Can I cancel my bid?

**No.** All bids are final. Think before you bid!

### What if the site goes down?

Your NFT is safe on the blockchain. You can access it from any NFT marketplace that supports Base.

### Do I own the copyright?

You own the NFT (the digital certificate). Copyright depends on what the artist specifies.

### How do I sell my NFT?

Once you win, the NFT is in your wallet. You can:
- List it on OpenSea
- Sell it on any NFT marketplace
- Transfer it to anyone
